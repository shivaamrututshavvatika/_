About Us

At Shivaamrut Utshav Vatika, we believe that the purest luxury is the sound of silence, the view of the mountains, and the taste of food grown from the very soil beneath your feet. Come for the view, stay for the peace, and leave with a soul full of memories.

Our Facilities

Organic Farming

Experience the "Farm-to-Table" lifestyle. Wander through our chemical-free gardens and enjoy fresh, seasonal produce harvested straight from the earth to your plate.

Ponds & Fishing

Find your zen by our tranquil waters. Whether you're looking for a quiet spot to meditate or want to try your hand at recreational fishing, our serene ponds offer the ultimate escape.

Mountain Views

Nature is your neighbor here. Every corner of the Vatika offers stunning, panoramic views of the majestic peaks, providing a breathtaking backdrop for your morning tea or evening stroll.

Photographic Zones

Capture the magic of your stay in our curated "picture-perfect" areas. From rustic landscapes to scenic overlooks, we provide the ideal setting for your memories and social media feeds.

Earth-Inspired Food

Taste the purity of nature. Our kitchen serves wholesome, traditional meals prepared with organic ingredients, mountain-fresh water, and a touch of local love.
